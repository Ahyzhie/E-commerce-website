Help & Support

To place an order, browse our products, select your items, adjust quantities, and click "Order Now". Your items will be added to the cart. Proceed to checkout from the Cart page.

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and online payments via PayPal. Payment details are processed securely.

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also contact us directly for updates.

We offer returns within 24 hours of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact our support team for assistance.

You can reach us via email at simply@delight.com or by phone at +6390 123 4567. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
