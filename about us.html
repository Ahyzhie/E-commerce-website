About Sweet Delights

Welcome to Sweet Delights, your premier bakery and cafe in the heart of Bontoc, Mountain Province. Founded in 2010, we have been delighting customers with freshly baked pastries, decadent cakes, and a refreshing selection of beverages for over a decade.

Our mission is to bring joy to every sip and bite, using only the finest locally sourced ingredients. From our flaky croissants and rich milk teas to our vibrant fruit drinks and specialty coffees, everything is crafted fresh daily to ensure the highest quality and flavor.

At Sweet Delights, we believe in fostering community and sustainability. We're proud to partner with local farmers and artisans, creating a welcoming space where friends, families, and students can gather, relax, and enjoy our expanded menu of baked goods and beverages.

Our Story

It all began with a group of friends bonded by their love for baking and brewing the perfect cup. Amid late-night study sessions fueled by homemade pastries and steaming coffee, we envisioned a cozy spot where people could unwind, connect, and savor life's simple pleasures.
